This is an experimental vote calculator to replace the Public Whip in feeding data into TheyWorkForYou.

Currently it works off the database dump of raw Public Whip data - but creating new calculated tables.

This will change to feed off TheyWorkForYou's division tables - and bring in the data from there for more Parliaments.

There are three main views:

  • Decisions - Decisions made collectively in the UK's Parliaments and Assemblies.
  • Policies - Grouping of decisions into related policies.
  • People - Members of the UK's Parliaments and Assemblies.
